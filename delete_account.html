Account Deletion Request

This page explains how users of EzRun can request deletion of their account and associated data. The deletion process is handled by Nishit Kumawat, the developer of EzRun.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your EzRun account, follow these steps:

Step 1: Send an email to: machmate.contact@gmail.com

Step 2: Use the subject line: “EzRun Account Deletion Request”

Step 3: Include the following details in the email:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your username (if applicable)
  • Confirmation that you want all associated data deleted

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Once a valid request is received, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • User account information (email, username)
  • Device connection associations
  • Usage logs related to your account
  • Any identifiers stored in our backend tied to your profile

What Data May Be Retained?

Certain data may be retained for security, auditing, or legal compliance:

  • Server logs (retained for up to 30 days)
  • Error or diagnostic logs that are not linked to personal identity

No data is ever sold or shared for marketing purposes.

Deletion Timeline

Once your request is received, deletion will be completed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is completed.

Contact

If you have any questions about the deletion process or data retention, contact us at:

Email: machmate.contact@gmail.com
