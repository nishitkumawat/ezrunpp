OTP Verification Guide

Last updated: 14/11/25

Welcome to EzRun ("the App"). This guide explains how to complete the OTP (One-Time Password) verification process to access all features of our App.

Verification Process

1 Initiate Verification

Open the EzRun app and navigate to the verification section. Enter your phone number with country code when prompted.

2 Request OTP

Tap the "Send OTP" button. Our system will automatically send a 6-digit verification code to your WhatsApp account associated with the phone number you provided.

3 Check WhatsApp

Open your WhatsApp application and look for a new message from EzRun containing your verification code. This may appear in your personal chats or business messages.

4 Enter OTP

Return to the EzRun app and enter the 6-digit code you received in the designated field. Make sure to enter it exactly as shown.

5 Complete Verification

Tap "Verify" to complete the process. Once verified, you'll have full access to EzRun's features and can begin using the app with your connected devices.

Important Notes

  • OTP codes are valid for 10 minutes from the time they're sent
  • You can request a new OTP if you don't receive one within 2 minutes
  • Make sure your phone has an active internet connection to receive WhatsApp messages
  • The phone number you enter must be registered with WhatsApp

Troubleshooting

If you don't receive the OTP:

  • Check that you entered the correct phone number with country code
  • Ensure your WhatsApp application is updated to the latest version
  • Verify that your phone has a stable internet connection
  • Check your WhatsApp spam or archived messages
  • Wait a few minutes and request a new OTP

Security Reminder

For your security:

  • Never share your OTP with anyone
  • EzRun will never ask for your OTP via phone call or other messaging platforms
  • If you receive multiple unexpected OTPs, contact us immediately
